What is DeFi?

DeFi(Decentralized finance) refers to financial services that run on smart contracts instead of relying middlemen such as bank or exchanges. DeFi's goal is to create a more accessible and transparent financial system that puts control back in the hands of the regular people.

DeFi vs Traditional finance

TradFi relies on middlemen. You need to rely on a bank to send money, earn interest, and get a loan. Banks make money by charging borrowers higher rates for loans than what they pay you. DeFi relies on code. You can send money, earn interest,and get a loan from the user through smart contracts that enforces the rules. Unlike banks, many Defi protocols are owned by users.So if you hold the protocols tokens, you get the upside instead of the some bank executive.
